The crypto market sentiment has pushed even further into extreme fear territory, with the Fear & Greed Index dropping to 14 — its lowest reading since late November 2025.

The index, which measures sentiment on a scale of 0 (maximum fear) to 100 (maximum greed), fell from 17 yesterday as Bitcoin tumbled below $73,000 and altcoins experienced double-digit losses.

"Extreme fear often signals a potential buying opportunity," notes the index methodology. "When investors are too fearful, that can be a buying signal."

The Fear & Greed Index analyzes several factors including volatility (25%), market momentum/volume (25%), social media sentiment (15%), Bitcoin dominance (10%), and Google Trends data (10%).

However, analysts caution that extreme fear can persist for extended periods during bear markets. The index remained below 25 for over three months during the 2022 crypto winter.
